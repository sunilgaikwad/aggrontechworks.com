About Us

Aggron Techworks is an engineering-driven firm specialising in high-precision manufacturing, stamping, machining and assembly. Our experienced engineering team uses modern CAD/CAM workflows, validated manufacturing processes and rigorous quality control to deliver robust components for automotive, industrial equipment and other demanding applications.

Our Expertise

Precision Machining

High-accuracy CNC & conventional machining for tight-tolerance components with superior surface finish.

Sheet Metal Stamping

Progressive & transfer stamping with optimized tooling to reduce stages and cycle time while maintaining strength.

Laser Cutting & Bending

Accurate laser cutting and press brake bending for complex sheet parts — fast turnaround and repeatability.

Design & Development

CAD/CAM (CATIA, SolidWorks, AutoForm) driven product development — from feasibility to production-ready design.

Assembly & Fabrication

Custom assemblies, jigs & fixtures, welding and final integration performed to industry quality standards.

Technical Support

Tooling validation, process optimization and long-term maintenance / production support for clients.
